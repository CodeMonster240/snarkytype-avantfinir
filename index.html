About SnarkyType

SnarkyType is a lightweight, delightful typing trainer built for speed, clarity, and a little attitude.

Typing sucks. That is why SnarkyType exists. Real scientists have proven that repetition is key to mastery. At SnarkyType, we are super repetitive, but to spice things up a bit, we add snarky comments to make you cry i mean laugh.

Design goals: low friction, high contrast, mean snark, and delightful motion. We optimize for keyboard-first workflows and mobile responsiveness.

  • Short focused drills (customizable)
  • Real-time accuracy and WPM
  • Snarky feedback
  • Offline-friendly and lightweight
